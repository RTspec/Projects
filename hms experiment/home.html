FAQ

Good to know
How do I apply for a room?

Click Book a Room and fill the online form. You’ll get an email with status updates.

What documents are required?

College ID, address proof, passport photo and fee payment receipt.

Can I change rooms later?

Yes. Submit a room‑change request from your dashboard. Approval depends on availability.

Is there a curfew?

Curfew is 10:00 PM for first‑year students; 11:00 PM for seniors. Late‑night passes are available.
